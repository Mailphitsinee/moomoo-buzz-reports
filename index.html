How Thai Investors Use LINE Daily
LINE TODAY — major news aggregation layer within LINE app; finance publishers like StockRadars post daily market close reports to millions of Thai users (confirmed: June 2 data published via LINE TODAY).

LINE OpenChat — popular among Thai retail investors for real-time stock discussion groups. Stocks are shared, debated, and "tip lines" circulate here. Private/invite-only; content not publicly accessible.

⚠️ SEC Warning: Multiple fake LINE OpenChat groups are impersonating licensed brokers. Always verify broker credentials on SEC Thailand's official portal before following any investment calls from LINE groups.
